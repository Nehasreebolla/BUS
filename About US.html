ABOUT US

Welcome to our website, your go-to source for up-to-date bus timings and alerts at the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad (IITH). We understand the importance of efficient and 

reliable transportation within the campus, and our goal is to provide you with accurate information about bus schedules to help you plan your journeys effectively. Whether you're a 

student, faculty member, or staff, our platform aims to simplify your daily commute by keeping you informed about the bus routes and notifying you of any changes or delays.

At our website, we have dedicated ourselves to creating a user-friendly experience that caters to the specific needs of the IITH community. Our team of developers and transportation 

experts have worked tirelessly to gather the most recent bus timings for different routes within the campus. We rely on real-time data to ensure that the information we present is 

current and reliable.

We take great pride in our alert system, which notifies you five minutes before the arrival of a bus at your desired stop. This feature is designed to help you stay on schedule and 

make the most of your time at IITH. By receiving timely alerts, you can plan your activities accordingly and avoid unnecessary waiting or rushing to catch a bus.

Our commitment to providing accurate and convenient bus information extends beyond just displaying schedules. We continuously strive to improve our services and incorporate user 

feedback to enhance the overall experience. Your satisfaction and ease of navigation are our top priorities, and we are open to suggestions on how we can better serve your needs.

As a dedicated team, we are passionate about facilitating smooth transportation on campus and ensuring that you have a hassle-free experience. We understand the significance of 

reliable bus services in maintaining a well-connected community, and we are here to support you every step of the way. Feel free to explore our website, familiarize yourself with the bus routes, 

and make the most of the alerts we provide.

Thank you for choosing our platform as your source of bus timings and alerts at IITH. We are excited to be part of your journey and look forward to serving you better in the future. 

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team. Happy commuting!
